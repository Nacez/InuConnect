Welcome to I&U Connect

I&U Connect connects university students with paid internships at partnering organizations, offering them valuable industry experience in their chosen fields.

Connecting University To Industry

Created by a Student, for Students

I&U Connect was founded with firsthand understanding of the challenges university students face when seeking meaningful industry experience. As a business created by a university student, we are committed to bridging the gap between education and employment by providing paid, multi-year internships that set students up for long-term success. Our platform is designed with students in mind.
